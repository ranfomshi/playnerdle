Benefits


Social

I'm not going to pretend like you are going to have word game parties or that word games will make you super popular at school or college but they are a great way to get to know people. Some of my earliest memories are playing Scrabble with my gran and watching a gameshow called Countdown with my family. According to swaay.com, You can gather new or old friends and play together in teams, which will make you all bond. Socializing has a lot of mental health benefits. It can improve your mood, according to research. There are various mental and physical health benefits for socializing, such as stress relief and relaxation.



Concentration

Concentration is a skill. And like with any skill, the more you practice the better you get and it can help with any other task that requires concentration. Just like conditioning a muscle. I always remember the old saying, "use it or lose it!".



Relaxation

Modern life brings with it a lot of stress and tension that needs relieving. Word games can result in hits of Dopamine, which is party responsible fro how we experience pleasure. You may have also noticed noticed how you can end up getting lost in a word game like Nerdle, where youare so focused that you can have a moment away from all of life's other stresses.



Vocabulary

The Nerdle dictionary of "answers" is around 5000 5 letter words. I am a qualified teacher and when I tried to think of how many 5-letter words I knew, I got a few hundred before I really started to struggle and had to rely on things like pluralised 4-letter words. So if a seemingly well-educated adult can stretch their vocabulary then I'd say pretty much anyone can. Furthermore, Nerdle will try to retrieve the word origin for you (if you guess a real word).
